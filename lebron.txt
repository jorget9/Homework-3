LeBron Raymone James (born December 30, 1984) 
is an American basketball player with the Los 
Angeles Lakers. James first garnered national 
attention as the top high school basketball 
player in the country. With his unique 
combination of size, athleticism and court vision, 
he became a four-time NBA MVP. After leading 
the Miami Heat to titles in 2012 and 2013, 
James returned to Cleveland and helped the 
franchise claim its first championship in 2016.
